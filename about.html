About Us

Who we are

We are a team comprised of senior business leaders and consultants with real-world experience. We leverage the extensive knowledge of our team to understand clients’ true needs and implement practical solutions.
As trusted advisors, the STRATOS team delivers expert knowledge and guidance built on years of experience across various sectors


On the Development side, STRATOS manages the Planning process, feasibility studies, develop an array of alternatives to develop forward-thinking transaction strategies and negotiate terms to produce the desired result.

Our preferences

We prefer to be involved early in the project life-cycle to evaluate the potential sources and uses of funds and help determine whether a project can be viably funded and paid for within reasonable time frames. We will educate the client about various structures of financing and based on feedback create a full financing package that can be used to competitively procure funding sources for a given project.

We have vast experience working with construction and permanent financing, mezzanine debt, equity sources, pledge lines of credit, and other institutional and non-institutional sources and can help structure and obtain tax-exempt and taxable bonds and notes for our clients’ projects.

Our Approach

Our Approach

The STRATOS approach is to focus on a review of the short term cash flow forecast, providing detailed analysis and proposed sensitivities on the Business Plan, based upon:

  • A review of future trends and the positioning of the business
  • Indicative valuation of the business as a whole and of its key component parts
  • Financial review based on detailed historical analysis
  • Practical commentary on the potential options available to the business, including refinancing, restructuring and potential insolvency
