Welcome To Stratos Capital

Who we are

Incorporated more than a decade ago, STRATOS is an investment advisory boutique dedicated to providing private market transactions and advisory solution in alternative investment.

We also provide intermediation in commodity trade finance. STRATOS has established a strong global network of private market investors and secondary market participants.

As an independent and management-owned company, STRATOS is able to offer its clients flexible and tailored solutions. Personal accountability of senior specialists assures quality of work, reliability and attention to details.

Our mission give solutions

We leverage the extensive knowledge of our team to understand clients’ true needs and implement practical solutions.


On the Development side, STRATOS manages the Planning process, feasibility studies, develop an array of alternatives to develop forward-thinking transaction strategies and negotiate terms to produce the desired result.

Core Team
