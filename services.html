Raising Capital

We have helped Businesses raise nearly every form of debt and equity capital— in markets that are both favourable and difficult. Our well-developed network of institutional investors can provide both capital and industry expertise, which enables us to help find the optimal investor for each client. At the outset, we get an understanding of the optimal capital structure for the business or project. This lays the foundation for the type and amount of funding sources that would be considered most appropriate in the circumstance, and these typically include any combination of debt, mezzanine finance and equity.

Strategic Advisory Services

Restructuring Advice
